Harry Potter, Mistborn, The Wheel of Time, The Mortal Instruments, The Hunger Games, Artemis Fowl, Percy Jackson etc. are my most favorite fantasy series. I love reading the novels of R.L. Stine, Enid Blyton, Dan Brown, Agatha Christie, Sidney Sheldon, Jules Verne, Isaac Asimov, Arthur C. Clarke, Muhammed Zafar Iqbal, Humayun Ahmed, Qazi Anwar Hussain, Khaled Hosseini etc. I listen to music whenever I can. Rock and metal music please me the most. Linkin Park, Avenged Sevenfold, Breaking Benjamin, Evanescence, System of a Down, Dead By April are a few of the bands that I adore. In my leisure, I also love cubing, making origamis. I’m a card magician as well. I’m a hobbyist programmer and a graphic designer. I found love in coding. Designing graphics, photo manipulation, making vector arts also fascinate me. When nothing works out for me, I just sit at my computer and start working on a random project. Be that a Python meme maker or a vector insect in Inkscape. I also love to work with motion graphics and animations.
